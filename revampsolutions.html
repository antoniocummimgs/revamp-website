Overview

At Revamp Solutions, we believe that stable housing is the foundation for rebuilding lives. We provide safe and affordable housing to individuals and families experiencing financial hardship. Whether you're facing temporary changes or long-term instability, we will work with you.

Our goal is to help you regain your independence and get on the path to self-sufficiency. We are here to support you every step of the way.

Our Services

  • Furnished Bedrooms – Comfortable and fully furnished.
  • Kitchen & Common Area – Shared spaces for cooking and relaxing.
  • Washer & Dryer – On-site laundry facilities.
  • Wi-Fi & Internet – Complimentary access to stay connected.
  • Paid Utilities – All essentials included.

We will provide support with getting access to essential community services and resources.

Who We Serve

We support individuals committed to rebuilding their lives through stable housing:

  • Those experiencing homelessness or unstable housing
  • Individuals leaving rehabilitation or recovery programs
  • Formerly incarcerated people re-entering society
  • Young adults aging out of foster care
  • Anyone needing temporary housing during life transitions
